Lasagna

Description

The warm cheesy high-caloric meal. Perfect for dinner or any other time of day. This most decadent of delicacies is sure to appease any entity tormenting you.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna Noodles
  • Cheese
    • Parmesan
    • Mozzarella
    • Ricotta
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Ground Meat
  • Spices

Steps

  1. Cook the noodles.
  2. Cook and mix the sauce and meat. Add spices if you want.
  3. In a baking pan, layer the noodles, meat sauce, and cheeses.
  4. Put it in the oven to bake.
  5. Take it out when your orange god appears and demands tribute.
